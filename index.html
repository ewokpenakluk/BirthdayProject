butterfly philosophy is often used to depict transformation, beauty, and new life. The metamorphosis process from egg to caterpillar, then to chrysalis, and finally to butterfly is seen as a symbol of change and development. Butterflies are also often associated with beauty, gentleness, and freedom due to their graceful colors and movements. Moreover, butterflies remind us of the importance of personal growth processes and positive changes in life.
